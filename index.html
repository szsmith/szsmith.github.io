Faculty · Researcher · Industry Practitioner

Gatton College of Business and Economics, University of Kentucky

I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, financial services, and energy, with a research focus on AI governance, IT audit, and emerging-technology risk. My work bridges the gap between deeply technical systems and the institutions that have to deploy, audit, and regulate them.

Before joining the University of Kentucky, I spent two decades in industry and federal research, including roles at Microsoft, Amazon Web Services, AT&T, PwC, and Bank of America, with research engagements through NSF and NIST. That combined vantage point of practitioner, auditor, and academic now informs how I approach the questions courts, conferences, and clients bring to me.
