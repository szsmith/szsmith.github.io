03 · Research and Writing

Areas of research

My peer-reviewed research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and the regulated industries that have to deploy these systems. A complete publication list is maintained on Google Scholar.

Adversarial AI and LLM Red-Teaming

Transferability of adversarial attacks across large language models, with emphasis on attacks generated on open-weight surrogate models transferring to closed-weight blackbox targets.

AI Governance for Critical Infrastructure

How AI systems deployed in energy, financial services, healthcare, and other regulated industries create governance gaps that existing audit and compliance frameworks were not designed to address.

Federal AI Compliance

NIST Risk Management Framework, FedRAMP, and Authority to Operate processes applied to AI systems. Direct expert input to NIST on the AI Risk Management Framework.

Energy Sector Cybersecurity

NERC CIP compliance, OT and IT convergence in utilities, and the regulatory and policy questions emerging at the intersection of renewables, distributed assets, and grid security.
